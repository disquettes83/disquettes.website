about disquette•s

I'm Luigi, aka disquette•s, an Italian music producer/beatmaker and DJ with over 15 years of experience creating electronic productions that perfectly capture the essence of the '80s but with a contemporary freshness.

my distinctive sound

You won't find the same clichés, but unique productions that sound incredibly good! If you're fascinated by the nostalgic sound of New Wave, Synth-pop, and Italo-disco that's been experiencing a renaissance in recent years, you're in the right place. I can bring these sounds and '80s aesthetics to your project or produce an original catchy track that stands out from the crowd.

specialization

I specialize in Retrowave, Synthwave, Dreamwave, Popwave, Chillwave, Nu-Disco, and Synth-pop, incorporating:

  • Vintage analog synthesizers and modern software emulations
  • Classic drum machines with that characteristic '80s punch
  • Atmospheres rich with nostalgic reverbs and delays
  • Arrangements that perfectly balance energy and space

services

  • Complete production of original tracks
  • Remixes and reinterpretations
  • Specialized mixing and mastering for electronic genres

how I work

Every project begins with an in-depth conversation about your influences, goals, and artistic vision. I offer constructive feedback and creative suggestions throughout the process, ensuring a collaborative experience that leads to exceptional results.
