about disquette•s

I'm Luigi, an Italian music producer crafting electronic music for over 15 years.

sound

My productions feature nostalgic New Wave, Synth-pop, and Italo-disco sounds reimagined with contemporary techniques. I create original tracks that stand out from typical retrowave clichés.

focus

Retrowave, Synthwave, and related genres with:

  • Nostalgic atmospheres and '80s-inspired arrangements
